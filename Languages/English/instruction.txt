First, processes of the brain will be examined under relaxation conditions. It is therefore very important in this part of the experiment that you relax as much as possible.\n During the recording of the resting EEG, you will be instructed to open or close your eyes. If you are instructed to open your eyes or to keep them open, please direct your relaxed gaze at the monitor.\n If you are instructed to close your eyes or to keep them closed, follow this instruction in a relaxed manner.\n To begin, press the space bar.
